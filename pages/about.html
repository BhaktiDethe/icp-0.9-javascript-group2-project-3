Adventure.Travel is Powered by ATTA

The Adventure Travel Trade Association (ATTA) is a vital leadership voice and partner for the adventure travel industry around the world. Established in 1990, the Adventure Travel Trade Association is the largest global network of adventure travel leaders. Our community is made up of 30,000 individual guides, tour operators, lodges, travel advisors, tourism boards, destination marketing and management organizations, outdoor educators, gear companies and travel media who share a belief and commitment to sustainable tourism. The connections and creativity of this vibrant community come together both virtually and in person to create and deliver the solutions that propel our businesses and our communities toward a responsible and profitable future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world, create unforgettable memories, and discover the beauty of different cultures.

At Travel Explorer, our mission is to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world and create unforgettable memories. Travel History

  • 2010:Foundation of Travel Explorer
    Description:Travel Explorer was founded with a mission to make travel accessible to everyone. The founders recognized the need for a travel service that catered to diverse needs and budgets, aiming to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world.
  • 2012:Launch of the First Website
    Description:The company launched its first website, marking a significant step in its digital presence. This platform allowed customers to browse and book customized travel packages tailored to their preferences, making the travel planning process more convenient.
  • 2015:Introduction of Eco-Friendly Travel Options
    Description:In response to growing environmental concerns, Travel Explorer expanded its services to include eco-friendly travel options. This initiative aimed to promote sustainable tourism practices, encouraging travelers to make environmentally conscious choices while exploring new destinations.
  • 2018: Partnership with Local Guides
    Description:Travel Explorer partnered with local guides in over 30 countries to enhance travel experiences. This collaboration allowed travelers to gain authentic insights into local cultures, traditions, and hidden gems, enriching their journeys and supporting local economies.
  • 2020:Virtual Travel Experiences Description:During the COVID-19 pandemic, Travel Explorer adapted to the changing landscape of travel by introducing virtual travel experiences. These online offerings allowed customers to explore destinations from the comfort of their homes, providing a unique way to experience travel during challenging times.
